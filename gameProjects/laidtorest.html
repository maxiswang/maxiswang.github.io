Until I Am Finally Laid To Rest is based on a set of characters I created to better categorize my intrusive thoughts. The story is a Choose-Your-Own-Adventure Visual Novel built in TyranoBuilder.

TyranoBuilder is already bit dense to work with, but I put in extra effort by looking into their programming language. This allowed me to do a more complex CYOA system, where your next scene depends on choices you make during the story. It functions like a personality quiz.

There are around 4k words in the whole story, but each playthrough will only cover around 1.5k words. There was a requirement to use photobashing on at least two characters, which I used for every character except The Sinful.
