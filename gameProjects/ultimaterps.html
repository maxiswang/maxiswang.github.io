The idea of Ultimate Rock Paper Scissors (Or Ultimate RPS for short) was simple: How many times can I force someone to play Rock Paper Scissors while playing a card game? The answer turned out to be quite a lot.

I was assigned to make a Trading Card Game, with at least 25 cards. I ended up designing 35 cards in total. Other than the cards themselves, I ended up developing the world of RPS further, giving a proper backstory to each playable master.

This card game takes inspiration from Inscryption, MTG, and Hearthstone.
